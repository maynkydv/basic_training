
-> Intialisation and Creation 

npx sequelize-cli seed:generate --name seed-users
npx sequelize-cli seed:generate --name seed-posts
npx sequelize-cli seed:generate --name seed-userData


-> apply all seed
npx sequelize-cli db:seed:all


-> apply any named seed
npx sequelize-cli db:seed --seed 20240926095735-seed-userData


-> undo seeder
npx sequelize-cli db:seed:undo --seed 20240926095735-seed-userData

npx sequelize-cli db:seed:undo:all
